Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application “BG Status Check” handles user information.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers.

Users may enter and save application (case) credentials for convenience. This information is stored locally on the user’s device only and is not accessible to the developer or any third parties.

Data Usage

The app uses the provided case information solely to request the application (case) status from the official website of the Ministry of Justice of the Republic of Bulgaria.

Data Storage

All user data, including up to 5 saved cases, is stored locally on the device.

The app does not transmit, store, or process this data on any external servers, except for sending requests to the official government website to retrieve case status.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use:

  • analytics services
  • advertising networks
  • tracking tools

Security

All data is stored locally on the user’s device. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
